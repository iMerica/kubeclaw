# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for contributors and maintainers.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Give actionable technical feedback.
- Help keep discussions focused and inclusive.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Trolling, baiting, or sustained disruption
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this code.

Repeated or severe violations can result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting Conduct Issues

Report incidents to: `conduct@kubeclaw.ai`

Include:
- what happened
- links/screenshots if available
- when it occurred

Reports are handled as confidentially as practical.
